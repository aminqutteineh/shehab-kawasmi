The collection of the artist Shehab Kawasmi. Shehab's work reminds us of orientalist paintings who wrote the history of Jerusalem from their own perspective. The beholder soon realizes that Shehab’s paintings reveal their Arab identity, taking us back in time to view Jerusalem in its utmost beauty and the artist’s frozen moments of the glorious past or the weeping over the relics of the past.
